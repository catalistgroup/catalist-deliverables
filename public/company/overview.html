What We Do

Wholesale distribution,
reimagined

Catalist Group connects brands with online sellers through a technology platform that eliminates the friction of traditional wholesale. We maintain direct relationships with 565+ brands across 42 product categories — from household names like Under Armour and Cuisinart to specialty brands like Stonewall Kitchen and Luigi Bormioli.

Our platform handles everything from product discovery and ungating to automated purchase orders and invoicing — all powered by AI agents that work around the clock.
